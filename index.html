ABOUT After the 2nd World War, Jean Prouve began designing temporary demountable houses for the war-victims in Lorraine and Franche-Comté.
Based on Jean Prouve's research and prototypes for demountable houses, this project focuses on the ease of construction and the multiplicity of possible programs, while seeking at thesame time a high standard of casual living.
